Please be advised that select rooms are currently undergoing enhancements to improve your overall stay experience. We appreciate your understanding and assure you that all efforts are made to minimize any inconvenience.

To Our Valued Customers:

  • Room Enhancements

    Some rooms are currently undergoing upgrades to offer you improved comfort and modern amenities. We’ve scheduled all works to ensure minimal disruption during your stay.

  • Enhanced Cleaning Protocol

    Your health and safety are our top priority. All rooms are thoroughly sanitized and maintained according to the latest hygiene standards.

  • Check-in and Check-out Update

    Check-in time is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be requested, subject to availability.

    Please note that all our rooms are strictly non-smoking to ensure a fresh and comfortable environment for every guest. We have designated place for smoking.
